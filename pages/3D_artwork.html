Competition placement

2018-2023



CO-LIVING QUARTERS was a design entry I did with Kio Oura for Saint-Gobain's architecture student contest 2022. The task site is located in east Warsaw, not far from Vistula river. The design proposal offers a diverse and versatile social living environment for students. The old and appreciated architecture is brought together with the new and innovative through deliberate massing and choice of materials. Existing spaces have been redesigned and complemented with additional spaces for public culture activities, creating a public plaza with which the residential buildings share the plot with.
